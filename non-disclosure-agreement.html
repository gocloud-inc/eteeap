Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

This Non Disclosure and Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Cloudmyx Co., hereinafter known as the "Disclosing Party", and the undersigned receiving party (the "Receiving Party"), and collectively both parties known as "Parties".

WHEREAS, the Disclosing Party shall disclose or deliver tangible or intangible information to the Receiving party that may be considered confidential or proprietary information;

WHEREAS, the Disclosing Party desires to be assured that the proprietary information owned by them shall not be disclosed to any third party without their consent and shall be maintained and protected by the Receiving Party;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants included in this agreement, both parties agree as follows

I. COMPANY’S TRADE SECRETS

During the course of the performance of Affiant’s duties in dealing with the Company, the Affiant has or will be exposed to the Company’s Confidential Information. “Confidential Information” is defined as information, material, or strategies that are commercially valuable to Company and not generally known or readily ascertainable in the industry. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • (a) technical information concerning the Company’s present and future products and services, including product know-how, formulas, designs, devices, diagrams, software code, hardware, test results, processes, inventions, research projects and product development, technical memoranda, and correspondence;
  • (b) information concerning the Company’s business, including cost information, profits, margins, sales information, accounting and unpublished financial information, business plans, markets and marketing methods, customer lists and customer information, purchasing techniques, supplier lists, supplier information, and advertising strategies
  • (c) information concerning the Company’s employees and contractors, including salaries, strengths, weaknesses, and skills;
  • (d) information made available by the Company’s customers, suppliers, employees, consultants, or co-venture partners with Company for study, evaluation or use, and
  • (e) any other information not generally known to the public which, if misused or disclosed, could reasonably be expected to adversely affect Company’s business;
II. NON-DISCLOSURE OF TRADE SECRETS

The Employee shall keep the Company’s Confidential Information, even if it is prepared, developed or otherwise by the Employee, in the strictest confidence. The Employee will not disclose such information to anyone outside the Company without the Company’s prior written consent, nor will Page 2 of 3 the Employee make use of any Confidential Information for the Employee’s own purposes or the benefit of anyone other than Company.

III. RETURN OF MATERIALS

When the Affiant’s dealings with the Company ends for whatever reason, whether voluntary or involuntary, the Employee will promptly deliver to the Company all originals and copies of all documents, records, software programs, media, databases, client listings, and other materials containing any Confidential Information. The Affiant will also return to the Company all equipment, files, software programs, and other personal property belonging to Company

IV. CONFIDENTIALITY INFORMATION SURVIVES EMPLOYMENT

The Affiant’s obligation to maintain the confidentiality and security of Confidential Information remains even after the Affiant’s dealings with the Company end, whether voluntary and involuntary, and continues for so long as such Confidential Information remains a trade secret
